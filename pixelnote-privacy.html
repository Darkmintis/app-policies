Privacy Policy for PixelNote

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

Overview

PixelNote ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how PixelNote handles information and permissions while providing a fully offline, privacy-first experience.

Data Collection

PixelNote does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any identifying information.
  • No Image Data: Images you create remain on your device; we never upload or store them.
  • No Text Data: Text content you enter is processed locally and never leaves your device.
  • No Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the app.
  • No Account Required: Registration or login is not needed.

Permissions

Camera

Purpose: To capture or embed images within the app. All images are stored locally; none are shared or transmitted.

Storage

Purpose: To save and read files necessary for app functionality. All files remain on your device.

Internet (Optional)

Purpose: Only used to display advertisements via Google AdMob. No personal or image data is transmitted by PixelNote. Ad information is collected solely by Google. See Google’s Privacy Policy for details.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

PixelNote may display ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect information such as device ID, IP address, OS version, and general location. This data is collected by Google, not by PixelNote.

Children's Privacy

PixelNote does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for users aged 13 and older.

Image Sharing

  • You choose which apps to share through (WhatsApp, Messenger, etc.).
  • Sharing is handled by your device's native functionality.
  • PixelNote does not access shared content after it leaves your device.

Data Security

All processing happens locally and no personal data is collected, so there is no risk of data breaches from our side.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Updates will appear on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.

Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at: darkmintis@gmail.com

Consent

By using PixelNote, you agree to this privacy policy.

Summary: PixelNote is a privacy-first offline app. No personal information, images, or text leave your device. The only third-party service is Google AdMob for ads (if enabled), governed by Google’s privacy policy.
