Privacy Policy for Tic Tac Toe

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

Overview

Tic Tac Toe ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how Tic Tac Toe handles information and permissions while providing a fully offline, privacy-first experience.

Data Collection

Tic Tac Toe does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

  • The game is fully offline; all gameplay data remains on your device.
  • Scores, settings, and preferences are stored locally and never shared.
  • No registration, login, or online account is required to play.

Permissions

The app requires no sensitive permissions. Internet access is used solely for displaying advertisements via Google AdMob.

Ads & Analytics

If this app displays ads, they may be provided by third-party ad networks such as Google AdMob. These networks may collect anonymous identifiers to personalize ads. See Google’s Privacy Policy for details.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any user data on external servers, there is no risk of data breaches from our side.

Children's Privacy

Tic Tac Toe does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is suitable for general audiences aged 3 and above.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Updates will be reflected on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, contact us at: darkmintis@gmail.com

Consent

By using Tic Tac Toe, you consent to this privacy policy.

Summary: Tic Tac Toe is a privacy-first offline game. No personal information, scores, or preferences leave your device. The only third-party service is Google AdMob for ads, which is governed by Google’s privacy policy.
